How to make a sandwich?

  1. Gather ingredients (e.g. bread, peanut butter, jelly)
  2. Choose two slices of bread
  3. Spread peanut butter on first slice of bread
  4. Spread a thin layer of jelly on second slice of bread
  5. Put the slices of bread together to form sandwhich
  6. Eat.
